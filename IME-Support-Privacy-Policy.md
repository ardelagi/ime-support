# Privacy Policy - IME Support Bot

**Last Updated:** February 17, 2026

## 1. Introduction

IME Support ("we", "us", "our", or "the Bot") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect information when you use our Discord bot.

By using IME Support, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Automatically Collected Information

When you use the Bot, we automatically collect:

- **Discord User IDs**: Your unique Discord identifier
- **Discord Server (Guild) IDs**: Server identifiers where the Bot is installed
- **Channel IDs**: Identifiers of channels where the Bot operates
- **Role IDs**: Role identifiers for permission management
- **Usernames and Discriminators**: Your Discord display name (for ticket management)
- **Message Content**: Content of messages in tickets and bot interactions
- **Timestamps**: When tickets are created, closed, and when messages are sent
- **Ticket Metadata**: Ticket numbers, status, assigned staff, ratings, and close reasons

### 2.2 User-Provided Information

Information you provide when:
- Creating support tickets
- Filling out forms
- Providing feedback or ratings
- Configuring Bot settings (server administrators)
- Responding to surveys or feedback forms

### 2.3 Server Configuration Data

For servers where the Bot is installed, we store:
- Bot configuration settings
- Custom welcome messages
- Panel configurations
- Permission settings
- Auto-close settings
- Support team configurations
- Custom integration settings

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

### 3.1 Core Functionality
- Create and manage support tickets
- Route tickets to appropriate staff members
- Store ticket transcripts and histories
- Provide ticket analytics and statistics
- Manage permissions and access control
- Send notifications and updates

### 3.2 Service Improvement
- Analyze Bot usage patterns
- Improve Bot features and performance
- Debug and fix technical issues
- Develop new features
- Generate anonymized usage statistics

### 3.3 Communication
- Send ticket updates and notifications
- Provide support and respond to inquiries
- Send important service announcements

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Storage Location
- Data is stored in secure PostgreSQL databases
- Ticket transcripts are stored in S3-compatible object storage
- Data is stored on servers in Indonesia

### 4.2 Security Measures
We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data:
- Encrypted data transmission
- Access controls and authentication
- Regular security updates
- Secure backup systems
- Limited access to data (only authorized personnel)

### 4.3 Data Retention
- **Active Tickets**: Stored while the ticket is open
- **Closed Tickets**: Retained based on server configuration (typically 30-90 days)
- **Transcripts**: Stored indefinitely unless deletion is requested
- **User Data**: Retained while you use the Bot
- **Server Data**: Retained while the Bot is in the server

## 5. Data Sharing and Disclosure

### 5.1 We Do NOT Sell Your Data
We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

### 5.2 When We May Share Data

We may share information only in these limited circumstances:

- **Server Administrators**: Server admins can access ticket data from their servers
- **Discord API**: Necessary data is shared with Discord to provide Bot functionality
- **Legal Requirements**: When required by law, court order, or legal process
- **Service Providers**: Trusted third-party services that help operate the Bot (e.g., hosting providers)
- **Safety and Security**: To protect rights, property, or safety of users

### 5.3 Third-Party Services

The Bot may interact with:
- Discord (required for operation)
- Cloud storage providers (for transcripts)
- Database hosting services
- Analytics services (anonymized data only)

## 6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights regarding your data:

### 6.1 Access
- Request access to your personal data
- Request ticket transcripts and history

### 6.2 Correction
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Update your information through ticket interactions

### 6.3 Deletion
- Request deletion of your data using the `/gdpr delete` command (if enabled)
- Request data export using the `/gdpr export` command (if enabled)
- Leave servers to stop data collection in those servers

### 6.4 Objection
- Object to processing of your data
- Opt-out of certain features (where applicable)

### 6.5 Data Portability
- Request a copy of your data in a portable format

## 7. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we discover that we have collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly.

## 8. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside of Indonesia, please be aware that your data may be transferred to and processed in Indonesia. By using the Bot, you consent to this transfer.

## 9. Cookies and Tracking

The Bot does not use cookies. However, the web dashboard may use:
- Session cookies (for login)
- Local storage (for preferences)
- Analytics (anonymized)

## 10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be:
- Posted on this page
- Effective immediately upon posting
- Announced in our support server (for major changes)

Your continued use of the Bot after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

In the event of a data breach that affects your personal information, we will:

- Provide information about the breach and steps taken
- Comply with applicable data breach notification laws
